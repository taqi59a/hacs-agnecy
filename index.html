About HACs L.A

HACs L.A (Handling Aviation & Cargo Services) is a professional travel agency based in Lubumbashi, Democratic Republic of Congo. We specialize in flight bookings, cargo handling, and comprehensive travel solutions.

Founded with a mission to make air travel accessible and affordable for everyone in the DRC, we work with major airlines including Ethiopian Airlines, Kenya Airways, South African Airways, RwandAir, and more. Whether you're traveling for business, family visits, or leisure — we guarantee the best prices and personalized service.
